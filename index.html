Jollof Rice Recipe

By Omolola Eyanuku: ALT/SOE/025/5119

Jollof rice is a delicios West AFrican dish cooked with rice, tomatoes, and spices. Read more about Jollof here

Recipe Information

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Cooking time: 45 minutes

Servings: 6 people

Difficulty level: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of long grain rice
  • chicken/ meat stock
  • 1 cup of blended tomatoes
  • 200ml vegetable oil
  • 1 Large chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice and set aside
  2. Heat the oil in a pot and fry the onions until soft and fragrant
  3. Add blended tomatoes, spices and seasoning cubes
  4. Cook until the sauce thickens
  5. pour in the chicken or meat stock and add water, then bring to a boil
  6. add the washed rice and let it cook on low heat

Notes

Tips to make your Jollof extra special

  • Let the rice sit on low heat for extra minutes before turning off the stove for extra smokiness.
  • You caan add fresh thinly sliced onions and tomatoes and let it steam for an extra 5 minutes
